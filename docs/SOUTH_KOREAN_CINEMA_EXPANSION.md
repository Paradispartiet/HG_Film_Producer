# South Korean cinema expansion

This catalogue batch adds the four South Korean films still missing from the agreed 98-film correction:

1. Peppermint Candy (1999)
2. Oasis (2002)
3. The Host (2006)
4. The Wailing (2016)

Together with Secret Sunshine (2007), Poetry (2010), and Burning (2018), which were already present from the modern-canon expansion, this completes the requested seven-film South Korean block.

## Catalogue integration

The films enter the same shared scenario chain used by:

- Production Cases
- Film Atlas
- Film Director
- Film School
- Film History
- Research Control Room

The catalogue grows from 282 to 286 unique Production Cases.

## Identity safety

Generic English titles are not accepted on title and year alone:

- Oasis must also match the original title Oasiseu or director Lee Chang-dong.
- The Host must also match the original title Gwoemul or director Bong Joon-ho.
- The Wailing must also match Goksung/Gokseong or director Na Hong-jin.

This prevents unrelated same-year films from being reused accidentally.

## Research status

All four cases remain provisional Production Cases:
