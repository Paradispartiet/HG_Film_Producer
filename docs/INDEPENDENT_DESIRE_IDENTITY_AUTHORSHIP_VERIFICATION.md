## Shared production question

The films form a comparison group around independent cinema's ability to make desire, identity and self-authorship visible through different production systems:

- genre collision, queer stigma and AIDS-era cultural conflict in *Poison*
- embodied adolescent viewpoint and British social realism in *Fish Tank*
- clothing, poetry, colour and social code-switching in *Pariah*
- autobiographical reconstruction, improvisation and artistic memory in *The Souvenir*
