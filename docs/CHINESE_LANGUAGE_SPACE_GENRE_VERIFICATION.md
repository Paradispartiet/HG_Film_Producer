## Comparative production systems

### Raise the Red Lantern

Repeated lantern-lighting, foot massage, meals, seasonal courtyards and controlled access transform a historical household into a visible machine of hierarchy. The 35mm colour system, frontal architecture, ensemble restraint, ritual sound and small editorial variations make power procedural rather than merely personal.

### A Touch of Sin

Four widely reported acts of violence are researched, geographically separated and rebuilt as linked stories of corruption, migration, service work and factory labour. Observational social space is repeatedly ruptured by crime, action and wuxia imagery without abandoning the documentary evidence beneath the fiction.

### The Assassin

A Tang-dynasty tale becomes a measured wuxia of landscape, layered interiors, fabric, air and moral hesitation. Long quiet passages and environmental sound are interrupted by short, sharply edited fights, shifting the genre from combat quantity toward attention and female agency.

### Long Day's Journey Into Night

An achronological memory noir gives way to a nocturnal dream presented through an approximately hour-long 3D sequence shot. Three cinematographers, traversable production design, continuous performance, sound orientation and the contrast between montage and duration make format change the film's central narrative event.

## Verification threshold

Each case now has:

- four inspectable HTTPS sources from at least two publishers;
- a complete 17-area history and craft audit;
- at least nine `source_verified` areas;
- explicit `mapped` or `not_central` status where department-level evidence is incomplete;
- one playable history match, one partial match and one mismatch;
- a verification record linked to the existing scenario ID.

## Sources

The batch uses institutional records, historical archive entries, programme notes and filmmaker interviews from the British Film Institute, La Biennale di Venezia, Festival de Cannes and Film at Lincoln Center.

Sources support only the registry's permitted areas: `overall`, `screenplay`, `cinematography`, `editing` and `sound`.

## Integration

The profiles are exposed through the already integrated film-history resolver rather than another branch in the large React panel. They use a dedicated four-film donor pool, so alternatives compare ritualised domestic space, researched social violence, historical wuxia attention and immersive memory geography rather than unrelated production systems.
