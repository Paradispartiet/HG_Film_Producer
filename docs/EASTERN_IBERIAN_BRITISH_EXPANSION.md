## Catalogue rules

The expansion reuses an existing Production Case only when year, title and original-title or director identity agree. Generic titles including `Daisies`, `Underground`, `Tabu`, `Naked` and `Hunger` have explicit false-positive tests.

Each film receives a film-specific provisional production blueprint covering screenplay, cinematography, editing, sound and learning. New records remain marked `needs_research` until source-backed historical and department-level verification is completed.

The catalogue test reports the exact pre-expansion count, genuinely missing count and final count in CI rather than assuming that all targets are new.
