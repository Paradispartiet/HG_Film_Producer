# Modern independent, Asian and prize-cinema expansion

## Decision

The following 27 requested films are added to HG Film Producer as the first modern-canon expansion after the 58-film historical-period expansion.

None of the 27 title/year pairs already existed in the 217-case catalogue. The complete Production Case catalogue therefore contains **244 cases** after this expansion.

- 44 cases retain completed source verification.
- 200 cases retain a complete 17-area audit while showing unfinished research honestly.
- Every new film receives a film-specific playable Production Case brief.
- Every new film enters Film History, Film Atlas, Film School matching and Research Control Room through the shared catalogue.
- The new cases remain `needs_research` / `research_pending` until their film-specific evidence review is complete.

The inclusion of a festival winner or canonized film does not itself verify production claims. Awards and reputation may guide research priority, but verification still requires inspectable historical and department-level sources.

## Learning comparisons added by the batch

The expansion gives the game new meaningful contrasts:

- videotaped confession versus observational community documentary;
- microbudget city wandering versus designed suburban alienation;
- Black historical memory versus Taiwan family chronicle;
- Taiwan urban modernism versus Chinese provincial duration;
- Japanese procedural horror versus Korean moral realism;
- realist digital landscape cinema versus folklore-driven bifurcated narrative;
- performance-developed British ensemble drama versus anti-classical digital musical melodrama;
- long-dialogue landscape chamber cinema versus embodied historical procedural suspense;
- activist archival montage versus postcolonial restitution essay film;
- conventional historical observation versus radical first-person historical adaptation.

These contrasts can supply plausible alternatives in the Production Case missions without turning wrong choices into nonsense.
