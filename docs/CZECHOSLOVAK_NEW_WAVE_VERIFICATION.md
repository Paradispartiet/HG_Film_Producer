# Czechoslovak New Wave verification

This batch continues source-backed quality control of existing Production Cases. It adds no films to the catalogue.

## Verified films

- Closely Watched Trains (1966)
- The Firemen's Ball (1967)
- Marketa Lazarová (1967)
- The Cremator (1969)

## Production systems compared

- literary wartime tragicomedy built from railway routine, sexual embarrassment and understated resistance;
- nonprofessional ensemble satire built from one social event, color observation and accumulating institutional failure;
- researched medieval historical modernism built from landscape, fragmented chronology, production design and layered sound;
- grotesque political horror built from subjective lenses, associative editing, controlled performance and ritualized music.

Each film has a source-backed history profile, a complete 17-area audit, three playable historical alternatives and four inspectable institutional or archival sources.

The unified verification registry reaches 80 source-backed Production Cases after this batch. Catalogue size remains dynamic and unchanged by this work.
