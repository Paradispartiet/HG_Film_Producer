# Learning-first product decision

HG Film Producer teaches film craft. Completion records where the player is in the material; it is not a grade.

Production Cases must not use points, best-result competition, prestige tiers or generic budget and schedule simulation. Film-specific production conditions may be taught when they are documented facts that explain how a film was made.

This decision governs future Production Case design and should be read together with `GAME_DIRECTION.md` and `PRODUCTION_CASE_LEARNING_MODEL.md`.
