# South and Southeast Asian catalogue expansion

This expansion adds the seven remaining films from the agreed South and Southeast Asian correction block:

1. Syndromes and a Century (2006) — Apichatpong Weerasethakul
2. The Lunchbox (2013) — Ritesh Batra
3. Norte, the End of History (2013) — Lav Diaz
4. Court (2014) — Chaitanya Tamhane
5. Marlina the Murderer in Four Acts (2017) — Mouly Surya
6. The Disciple (2020) — Chaitanya Tamhane
7. Return to Seoul (2022) — Davy Chou

## Catalogue effect

The shared catalogue grows from 286 to 293 unique Production Cases.

Every case receives a film-specific provisional blueprint for:

- screenplay and dramatic structure
- image and spatial design
- editing and duration
- sound and music
- production-learning goals

The cases flow through the shared catalogue used by Production Cases, Film Atlas, Film Director, Film School, Film History and Research Control Room.

## Identity policy

All seven definitions use original-title or director confirmation when reusing an existing title-and-year match. This prevents generic English titles such as Court, The Lunchbox and The Disciple from absorbing unrelated films.

## Research status

These are playable catalogue cases, not completed research records. They remain marked:

- `manual_south_southeast_asian_case_needs_source_verification`
- `needs_research`

Source-backed film history, department-level craft evidence, technical specifications, festival context and legacy claims must be added through the separate verification workflow.

## Integrity requirements

The automated tests require:

- exactly seven unique new definitions
- no matches in the preceding 286-case catalogue
- exactly one resolved case per requested film
- complete playable brief targets for every film
- rejection of unrelated same-year generic-title cases
- exactly 293 unique scenario IDs after merging
