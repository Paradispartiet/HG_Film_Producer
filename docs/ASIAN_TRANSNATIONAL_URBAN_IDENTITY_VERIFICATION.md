## Comparative production systems

### Cyclo

A stolen cycle-rickshaw opens into debt, coercion, prostitution and gang labour across post-Đổi Mới Saigon. A 35mm France-Vietnam production combines street observation, saturated colour, bodies, smoke, pop music and hallucinatory rupture so criminal violence grows from the same environment as daily work.

### Happy Together

Two Hong Kong men repeat separation, care and conflict while displaced in Buenos Aires. Wong Kar-wai, Christopher Doyle and William Chang use monochrome and saturated 35mm colour, cramped migrant interiors, road imagery, voice-over, tango and temporal ellipsis to make exile the form of the relationship.

### Infernal Affairs

A police mole inside the triads and a triad mole inside the police are constructed as mirrored information systems. Widescreen urban geometry, star performance, parallel editing, phones, recordings and coded communication replace continuous gunplay with exact pressure over who knows each identity.

### Return to Seoul

A French adoptee moves through South Korea and nearly a decade of changing relationships, jobs and self-presentations. A real adoptee journey, Park Ji-min's first screen performance, multilingual encounters, mobile urban observation and major temporal ellipses prevent return from resolving into one recovered origin.

## Verification threshold

Each case now has:

- four inspectable HTTPS sources from at least two publishers;
- a complete 17-area history and craft audit;
- at least nine `source_verified` areas;
- explicit `mapped` or `not_central` status where department-level evidence is incomplete;
- one playable history match, one partial match and one mismatch;
- a verification record linked to the existing scenario ID.

## Sources

The batch uses film records, historical archives, restoration material, programme notes and filmmaker interviews from the British Film Institute, La Biennale di Venezia, Festival de Cannes, Hong Kong Film Archive, Busan International Film Festival, the Criterion Collection and Film at Lincoln Center.

Sources support only the registry's permitted areas: `overall`, `screenplay`, `cinematography`, `editing` and `sound`.

## Integration

The profiles are exposed through the already integrated film-history resolver rather than another branch in the large React panel. They use a dedicated four-film donor pool, so alternatives compare Saigon labour and criminal coercion, Buenos Aires queer exile, Hong Kong double-undercover identity and Seoul adoptee return rather than unrelated regional systems.
