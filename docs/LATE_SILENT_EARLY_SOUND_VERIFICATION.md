# Late silent and early sound verification

This batch continues source-backed quality control of existing Production Cases. It adds no films to the catalogue.

## Verified films

- The Passion of Joan of Arc (1928)
- Man with a Movie Camera (1929)
- M (1931)
- City Lights (1931)

## Production systems compared

- close-up silent modernism that sacrifices stable geography for psychological confrontation;
- reflexive city-symphony documentary exposing camera, editor, projection and montage;
- early sound crime procedure using offscreen voices, silence, whistle and sound bridges;
- silent pantomime deliberately preserved after talkies, with synchronized score and selective effects.

Each film has a source-backed history profile, a complete 17-area audit, three playable historical alternatives and four inspectable institutional or archival sources.

The unified verification registry reaches 56 source-backed Production Cases after this batch. Catalogue size remains dynamic and unchanged by this work.
