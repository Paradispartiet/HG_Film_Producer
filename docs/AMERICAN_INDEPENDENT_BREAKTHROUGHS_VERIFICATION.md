# American independent breakthroughs verification

## Scope

This source-control batch verifies four existing playable Production Cases. It does not add films:

- `scenario_killer_of_sheep_1977`
- `scenario_sex_lies_and_videotape_1989`
- `scenario_slacker_1990`
- `scenario_daughters_of_the_dust_1991`

## Historical comparison

The batch compares four distinct American independent production systems:

1. an extended 16mm L.A. Rebellion production made on spare weekends in Watts;
2. a compact Louisiana chamber drama moving from festival discovery to specialty distribution;
3. an Austin microbudget city film structured through geographic and conversational handoffs;
4. a 35mm Black feminist historical feature joining Gullah research, nonlinear family narration and designed color.

## Verification requirements

Each case includes:

- a source-backed historical profile;
- all 17 history and craft areas;
- at least nine source-verified areas;
- one playable match, one partial match and one mismatch;
- four inspectable sources from independent institutional, archival, trade or filmmaker records;
- no generic budget, prestige, rank or reward mechanics.
