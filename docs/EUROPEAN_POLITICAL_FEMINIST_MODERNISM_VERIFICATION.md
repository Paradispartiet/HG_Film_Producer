# European political and feminist modernism verification

Verified 2026-07-20 as one source-backed Production Case batch:

- `scenario_cleo_from_5_to_7_1962`
- `scenario_the_conformist_1970`
- `scenario_jeanne_dielman_1975`
- `scenario_beau_travail_1999`

The batch compares four distinct production systems:

1. French Left Bank near-real-time city cinema built from Paris geography, mirrors, documentary observation and live song;
2. Italian political modernism built from fractured memory, Fascist architecture, expressive design and Storaro's colour-and-shadow system;
3. Belgian feminist durational cinema built from fixed framing, complete domestic labour, direct sound and minute deviations in routine;
4. French postcolonial body cinema built from Djibouti locations, military choreography, fragmented memory, music and landscape.

Each case contains four inspectable institutional, archival, trade or filmmaker sources, a complete 17-area audit, at least nine source-verified areas and a playable comparison using the other three real production systems.

The unified verification registry increases from 80 to 84 Production Cases. No new films were added to the catalogue.
