## Identity safety

Black Rain (1989) is explicitly identified as Shōhei Imamura's Kuroi ame. The matcher requires the correct original title or director, preventing accidental reuse of Ridley Scott's different film with the same English title and release year.

The same director-aware protection is applied to the generic English titles After Life and Monster.
