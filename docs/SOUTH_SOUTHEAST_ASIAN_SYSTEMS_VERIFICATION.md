## Comparative production systems

### Syndromes and a Century

A 35mm hospital diptych transforms memories of Apichatpong Weerasethakul's doctor parents into altered rural and Bangkok institutions. Repetition, architecture, changing light and the move from birdsong to mechanical hum make memory and urbanisation formally playable.

### Court

Lower-court and protest-music research becomes an anti-spectacle legal drama built on real Mumbai locations, sync sound, a predominantly nonprofessional cast, long static scenes and dozens of exact takes rather than improvised courtroom climax.

### Marlina the Murderer in Four Acts

A Cannes-developed Indonesian co-production rebuilds the western across four acts. West Sumba geography, formal tableaux, sparse landscape sound, deadpan violence and women helping one another shift revenge mythology toward a widow's agency and institutional abandonment.

### The Disciple

Real Hindustani musicians perform and record through extended takes while digital capture, lens changes, low-key lighting and rare spiritual camera movement follow an artist from youthful idealism toward ordinary disillusionment.

## Verification threshold

Each case now has:

- at least four inspectable HTTPS sources from at least two publishers;
- a complete 17-area history and craft audit;
- at least nine `source_verified` areas;
- explicit `mapped` or `not_central` status where department-level evidence is incomplete;
- one playable history match, one partial match and one mismatch;
- a verification record linked to the existing scenario ID.

## Sources

The batch uses records and interviews from the British Film Institute, Film at Lincoln Center, Criterion Collection, La Biennale di Venezia Historical Archive, International Film Festival Rotterdam, Museum of Modern Art, Quinzaine des cinéastes, Festival de Cannes Cinéma de Demain, Busan International Film Festival, ArtsEquator and American Cinematographer.

Sources support only the registry's permitted areas: `overall`, `screenplay`, `cinematography`, `editing` and `sound`.

## Integration

The profiles are exposed through the already integrated film-history profile resolver. They use a dedicated four-film donor pool, so historical alternatives compare mirrored institutions, legal realism, feminist regional genre and musical process rather than unrelated systems.
