## Case depth

Every film receives a distinct playable blueprint for:

- premise and dramatic structure
- image and spatial strategy
- editing and temporal organization
- sound and musical method
- production learning goals

The cases are available through the shared catalogue used by Production Cases, Film Atlas, Film Director, Film School, Film History and Research Control Room.

## Identity safety

A matching title and year are not sufficient for reuse. The matcher also requires the correct original title or director. This protects generic or translated titles such as `Yol`, `Central Station`, `Vera Drake` and `The Wrestler` from absorbing unrelated same-year films.

## Research status

These are complete playable mappings, not source-verified historical records. Each case remains marked:

- `manual_festival_winner_1981_2009_case_needs_source_verification`
- production brief verification status `needs_research`

Unsupported claims about awards, technical specifications, influence or production history are deliberately excluded until the research workflow verifies them.
