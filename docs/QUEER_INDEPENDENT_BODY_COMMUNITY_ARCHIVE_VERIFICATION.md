# Queer independent body, community and archive verification

This batch converts four existing playable scenarios from provisional mapping to source-backed Production Cases:

- `scenario_desert_hearts_1985`
- `scenario_paris_is_burning_1990`
- `scenario_safe_1995`
- `scenario_the_watermelon_woman_1996`

## Production systems

| Film | Verified production system |
| --- | --- |
| *Desert Hearts* | Independently financed lesbian period romance using Reno geography, intimate performance, country music and affirmative dramatic closure. |
| *Paris Is Burning* | Long-term community documentary built from ballroom performance, direct testimony, house structures, 1.33 observation and participant-defined language. |
| *Safe* | Low-budget 35 mm independent modernism using distant framing, controlled design, unresolved illness and environmental sound to turn affluent safety into threat. |
| *The Watermelon Woman* | Black lesbian hybrid feature combining romantic comedy, direct address, video, 16 mm, improvisation and fabricated archive as film-historical method. |

## Source threshold

Each film has four inspectable HTTPS sources from at least two publishers. The source set prioritizes:

- film institutes and preservation bodies;
- official distributor or collection records;
- filmmaker interviews;
- archive and restoration documentation.

Principal institutions include the Sundance Institute, UCLA Film & Television Archive partnerships, British Film Institute, Criterion Collection, American Film Institute, Sony Pictures Classics and the Library of Congress.

## Coverage

Each profile resolves all 17 film-study areas. At least nine areas per film are marked `source_verified`; unsupported lighting, costume or documentary details remain `mapped` or `not_central` rather than being promoted beyond the inspected evidence.

## Integration

The batch adds:

- four `FilmHistoryProfile` records;
- one controlled donor group, `queer_independent_body_community_archive`;
- one four-film verification record batch;
- dedicated map and choice tests;
- registry and global-count integration.

Verified Production Cases increase from **224 to 228**. No new scenarios are introduced.
