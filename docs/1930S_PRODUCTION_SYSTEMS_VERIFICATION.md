# 1930s production systems verification

This batch continues source-backed quality control of existing Production Cases. It adds no films to the catalogue.

## Verified films

- King Kong (1933)
- Modern Times (1936)
- Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs (1937)
- The Rules of the Game (1939)

## Production systems compared

- RKO creature spectacle integrating stop motion, miniatures, live action, editing and symphonic sound;
- independent Chaplin pantomime using speech and effects selectively as part of industrial satire;
- classical feature animation organized through story, character, background, effects, camera, ink-and-paint and sound departments;
- French ensemble staging using depth, long takes, mobile framing, layered dialogue and shared social space.

Each film has a source-backed history profile, a complete 17-area audit, three playable historical alternatives and four inspectable institutional or archival sources.

The unified verification registry reaches 60 source-backed Production Cases after this batch. Catalogue size remains dynamic and unchanged by this work.
