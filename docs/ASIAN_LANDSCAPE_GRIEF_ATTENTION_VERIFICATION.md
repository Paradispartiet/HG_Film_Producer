# Asian landscape, grief and moral attention Production Case verification

Verified on 2026-07-23. This batch reviews existing playable scenarios only; it adds no films.

## Cases

- `scenario_tropical_malady_2004`
- `scenario_still_life_2006`

- `scenario_poetry_2010`

## Comparative production systems

### Tropical Malady

A tender rural romance breaks in two and returns as a nearly wordless jungle pursuit. Locked 35mm compositions, darkness, animal sound, uncertain point of view and Thai spirit lore transform desire into a sensory relation among soldier, lover and tiger rather than continuing the first half through ordinary narrative causality.

### Still Life

Two searches cross Fengjie while demolition races ahead of the Three Gorges reservoir. Lightweight digital capture, real labour, widescreen geography, industrial sound, parallel journeys and brief surreal ruptures make redevelopment both a physical historical process and an intimate form of displacement.

### Secret Sunshine

A widowed mother moves from Seoul to Miryang, where town routines, religious community and catastrophic loss repeatedly overturn any stable idea of recovery. Supple widescreen observation, ordinary sunlight, restrained editing, environmental sound and Jeon Do-yeon's performance keep faith and forgiveness morally unresolved.

### Poetry

An older woman studies poetry while memory loss and her grandson's participation in sexual violence force beauty into conflict with responsibility. Han River landscapes, care work, social negotiation, patient observation, quiet sound and Yoon Jeong-hee's performance make attention both an artistic practice and an ethical burden.

## Verification threshold

Each case now has:

- four inspectable HTTPS sources from at least two publishers;
- a complete 17-area history and craft audit;
- at least nine `source_verified` areas;
- explicit `mapped` or `not_central` status where department-level evidence is incomplete;
- one playable history match, one partial match and one mismatch;
- a verification record linked to the existing scenario ID.

## Sources

The batch uses official festival archives, film-institute records, restoration catalogues, production reporting and filmmaker profiles from Festival de Cannes, La Biennale di Venezia, British Film Institute, Film at Lincoln Center, The Criterion Collection, Korean Film Council and The New Yorker.

Sources support only the registry's permitted areas: `overall`, `screenplay`, `cinematography`, `editing` and `sound`.

## Integration

The four profiles are exposed through the existing player-facing independent-storytelling resolver and use a dedicated donor pool. Alternatives compare queer mythic transformation, disappearing-landscape digital realism, unresolved spiritual grief and poetry as moral attention rather than unrelated Asian films.
